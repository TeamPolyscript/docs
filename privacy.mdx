## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Polyscript collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our Service. We collect the minimum required to operate the platform and never sell your data.

## What we collect

### Account data

Email address, handle, display name, and (if you sign in with Google) your Google account ID and avatar.

### Usage data

Logs of API requests, strategy executions, errors, and aggregated performance metrics. We use these to keep the Service reliable and to debug issues you report.

### Device data

When you connect the desktop app, we store a scoped device token plus device label and approximate region (for security alerts). You can revoke any device at any time from your profile.

## How we use it

- Operate, maintain, and improve the Service
- Authenticate you and protect your account
- Notify you of security events and material changes
- Comply with legal obligations

## Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with infrastructure providers strictly necessary to deliver the Service (hosting, email delivery, error monitoring), bound by data processing agreements.

## Cookies & tokens

We use an httpOnly session cookie for authentication. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. We may use first-party analytics that respect Do Not Track signals.

## Data retention

Account data is retained while your account is active and for up to 90 days after deletion (then permanently erased, except where legal retention requirements apply).

## Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these, email <a href="mailto:privacy@polyscript.io">privacy@polyscript.io</a>.

## Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach our privacy team at <a href="mailto:privacy@polyscript.io">privacy@polyscript.io</a>.
